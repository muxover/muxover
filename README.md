## About Me

I'm **Jax** — full name **Yousef Khaled**, though most people know me online as **@muxover**. I'm an Egyptian developer and medical student, and the founder of **Jax Development LLC**. I've always been curious about how things work, whether it's a complex tech system or the human body, and that curiosity drives everything I do.

Medicine and technology might seem very different, but they complement each other perfectly. Medicine teaches me precision and discipline, while development pushes me to think creatively and solve problems in new ways. Combining these two fields gives me a unique perspective for tackling challenges from multiple angles.

I started coding in 2019 with simple scripts, and over time it's grown into a true passion. Today, I build full-stack applications, work with cloud infrastructure, and create automation tools. I enjoy learning new technologies, experimenting with ideas, and turning them into practical projects that actually work.

---

## Company · Jax Development LLC

**Jax Development LLC** is my product-focused company, where I turn ideas into practical software solutions. Unlike traditional agencies, the company focuses on building its own products, from B2B systems to SaaS platforms, aimed at solving real-world problems and making workflows simpler and more efficient. Every project reflects a commitment to quality, innovation, and usability, ensuring that the tools we create are not just functional, but genuinely useful. Operating as an extension of my own development work, the company allows me to explore new technologies, experiment with ideas, and build software that addresses actual business needs — all while maintaining full control over the vision, design, and execution of each product.
